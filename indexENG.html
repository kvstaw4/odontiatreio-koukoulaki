About Us

Our clinic has been operating since 1981 in Heraklion, Crete, initially from Stavros and Mary and from 2011 and Savina. Our many years of experience, our continuous training, our human interest in our patients and our professional consistency guarantee the good Therapeutic effect on the various dental problems we face. Our clinic fully undertakes the dental care of adults and children. Our aim is to address all oral problems and restore the oral health of our patients.

Our Services

Cosmetic Dentistry

Our clinic fully undertakes the aesthetic restoration of each patient's smile. Broken teeth, gaps and other situations that make it difficult to smile, can be restored to improve facial aesthetics.

General Dentistry

Our clinic fully undertakes the dental care of adults and children. Our aim is to address all oral problems and restore the oral health of our patients.

Dental Implants

With dental implants, patients regain teeth firmly and aesthetically, so that the chewing function, speech and aesthetics of the face are restored very satisfactorily.

Prosthetic Dentistry

Prosthodontics is the field of dentistry that focuses on prosthetic restorations with the shape and appearance of normal teeth.
